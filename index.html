Welcome to my Portfolio

Hello! I'm Vanessa Berghoff

I am a student in graphic design and web development. In this porfolio, you will find examples of my work in Adobe Creative Cloud and HTML. I am a learning experience designer with an interest in learning more about the graphic design and web development fields.

As an LXD, solving problems with creative solutions has always been a passion of mine. I use instructional design as a means to blend expression and "out of the box" thinking with the right information and high-quality production.

My goal is to turn what some people would think of as a boring, compliant experience into one that surprises, delights, engages, and most importantly, delivers results.

These are the pillars of my work. I hope you enjoy.

What I Value Most

Here's how I make sure every learning solution I create is in its best possible form.

  • Impeccable quality. I am organized, detail-oriented, and efficient. It's in my blood to go above and beyond dotting my i's and crossing my t's.
  • Fluid collaboration & partnership. I am a thorough learning partner and collaborator. The journey is just as important as the destination. I value communication and transparency with stakeholders in the design process.
  • Tailored design. Each project requires a unique solution based on the challenge, context, timeline, or preference. Whatever the situation, I strive to find the right fit.
